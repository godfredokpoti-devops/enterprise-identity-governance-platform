Access Review Best Practices

  • Quarterly access certification for privileged accounts
  • Disable dormant accounts after 30 days
  • Require business justification for privileged access
  • Enforce manager approval for elevated permissions
  • Log every certification decision for audit evidence
  • Notify reviewrs seven days before certification expires
  • Immediately revoke access for terminated users or orphaned accounts
  • Export review evidence for SOX, ISO 27001, PCI DSS, and audit readiness
